# Welcome to PartnerFlow

PartnerFlow is a comprehensive service management platform that connects manufacturers, service providers, and customers through structured, automated service workflows. Whether you are managing warranty claims, field service operations, or partner networks, PartnerFlow gives every party the visibility and tools they need.

## What PartnerFlow Does

PartnerFlow lets you design exactly how a service moves from creation to completion. You define the stages, the rules that control progression between them, the data to collect, and the pricing to apply — then share that service with your partner network. Service records are created, routed, tracked, and invoiced automatically based on the flow you built.

## Three Types of Users

### Manufacturers / Service Creators
Build service flows, define quality standards, set up contracts, and manage a network of service providers. You have full visibility into every service record across all your partners.

### Service Providers
Receive and execute services shared by manufacturers. Access stages, documentation, inventory, and accounting tools needed to complete each case. Progress is tracked in real time.

### Customers
Access a branded customer portal to track service progress, approve estimates, confirm logistics, and receive updates — without needing a full platform account.

## Key Platform Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Service Flow Builder** | Design multi-stage workflows with transitions, conditions, and automations |
| **Partner Management** | Invite providers, share services, manage contracts and access |
| **Service Records** | Create, assign, and track individual service cases through the entire lifecycle |
| **Inventory** | Track parts usage, set thresholds, and manage purchase orders |
| **Accounting** | Generate estimates and invoices automatically from the service flow |
| **Customer Portal** | White-label portal for customer tracking and approvals |
| **Integrations** | Connect Gmail, logistics providers, CRMs, and automation tools |
| **API** | Full REST API for custom integrations and automation |
