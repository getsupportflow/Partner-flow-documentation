# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about PartnerFlow. If you cannot find what you are looking for, [contact our support team](/docs/support).

## General

### What is PartnerFlow?

PartnerFlow is a service management platform that connects manufacturers, service providers, and customers through structured, automated service workflows. It streamlines the entire service lifecycle — from case creation and partner assignment through to invoicing and completion — in one platform.

### Who can use PartnerFlow?

The platform serves three main user types:

- **Manufacturers / Service Creators** — build and manage service flows, partner networks, and contracts
- **Service Providers** — receive and execute services, track inventory, manage cases
- **Customers** — track their service case status through a branded customer portal

### Is the platform secure?

Yes. PartnerFlow uses:

- End-to-end encryption for all data in transit
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Role-based access controls
- Multi-factor authentication support
- Full audit logging of all user actions

### How much does it cost?

Pricing is subscription-based and varies by usage and feature tier. Contact the sales team for detailed pricing information.

## For Customers

### How do I track my service case?

You will receive a link to the customer portal when your service record is created. Use that link (and the verification sent to your email) to access real-time status updates, approve estimates, and confirm collection or delivery arrangements.

### What actions can I take in the customer portal?

Depending on how the service is configured by the manufacturer, you may be able to:

- View the current stage and status of your case
- Approve or request changes to an estimate before work begins
- Confirm a collection or delivery date
- Make a payment for out-of-warranty services
- Provide feedback when the service is complete

### Can I cancel or modify a service request?

Contact the service provider or manufacturer through the portal messaging feature. Once a case has been assigned to a provider, modifications require their agreement.

### What file types can I upload as supporting evidence?

Supported formats include:

- Images: JPG, PNG
- Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX
- Maximum file size: 10 MB per file

## For Service Providers

### How do I start receiving service cases?

1. Your administrator connects with the manufacturer in PartnerFlow
2. The manufacturer shares the relevant service with your organisation
3. You review the service criteria, sign the contract, and accept
4. Cases are assigned to your organisation as they are created — you receive notifications when new records arrive

### How do I update a service record's status?

Open the record from your dashboard and click the appropriate transition button. You may need to complete required fields before the transition is available. Every status change is logged automatically.

### How are invoices generated?

Invoices are created automatically based on the pricing rules in the service flow. As your team moves a case through stages, charges accumulate. At the end of the service (or billing period), PartnerFlow generates the invoice — you do not need to calculate or submit it manually.

### Can I integrate PartnerFlow with my existing systems?

Yes. PartnerFlow provides a full REST API and supports integrations with email, logistics providers, CRM systems, and automation platforms. Contact your manufacturer or the PartnerFlow support team for integration assistance.

## For Manufacturers

### How do I set up my first service?

Follow the [Setup Guide](/docs/getting-started/setup) to configure your company profile and users, then follow [Your First Service](/docs/getting-started/first-service) to build and publish your first service flow.

### What analytics are available?

The dashboard provides:

- Service record volume and status overview
- Stage bottleneck analysis
- Service provider performance metrics
- Inventory usage and purchase order tracking
- Financial summaries (estimates, invoices, payments)

### Can I customise the customer portal with my branding?

Yes. The customer portal can be configured with your company logo, brand colours, and a custom URL. Customers see your brand, not PartnerFlow.

### How do I manage which users at a partner organisation can access my services?

Share the service with the partner company. The partner's administrator then controls which of their users can access it via their own **Settings → Users** configuration.

### What integration options are available?

PartnerFlow integrates with:

- Email providers (Gmail, SMTP)
- Logistics and courier services
- CRM platforms
- Accounting software
- Automation tools (n8n, webhooks)
- Custom systems via the REST API

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

All modern browsers are supported: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Follow the reset link sent to your inbox

### Is my data backed up?

Yes. PartnerFlow maintains real-time data replication, daily automated backups, geographic redundancy, and 30-day backup retention.
