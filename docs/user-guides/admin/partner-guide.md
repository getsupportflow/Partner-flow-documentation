# Partner Guide

**Audience:** Service Provider (Partner)

This guide explains how to find manufacturer partners, accept service invitations, access shared services, and manage contracts — all from the Partners section of PartnerFlow.

## Find and Request Partnerships

1. Go to **Partners → Directory**
2. Search for the manufacturer by company name
3. Click **Request Partnership**
4. Monitor **Partners → Requests** for status updates

When the manufacturer accepts your request, the partnership becomes active and they can begin sharing services with you.

## Accept Partnership Invitations

If a manufacturer invites you directly:

1. Check **Partners → Requests** or your notifications
2. Click the invitation to review the request
3. Click **Accept**

## Access Shared Services

Once connected to a manufacturer:

1. Go to **Partners → Shared Services**
2. View all services shared with your company
3. Click into any service to review stages, transitions, custom fields, documentation, and contract terms before accepting

If a service requires formal acceptance and signing:

1. Open the service from **Partners → Awaiting Approval**
2. Review the full service criteria
3. Add comments or request clarification if needed
4. Click **Sign** to accept — an e-signature or acceptance flow opens
5. A verification link is sent to your registered email — complete this step
6. The manufacturer countersigns to finalise

## Manage Contracts

1. Go to **Partners → Contracts**
2. Select a pending contract and review the terms
3. Click **Sign** (e-signature or acceptance flow)
4. Download a copy for your records

All signed contracts remain accessible in **Partners → Contracts** for future reference.

## Manage Team Access to Shared Services

Not all team members need access to all partner services. Admins can control which users can work on specific shared services:

1. Go to **Settings → Users**
2. Select the user
3. Under **Service Access**, toggle access for each shared service

This ensures technicians only see the cases and workflows relevant to their role.

## Receiving Service Records

Once a service is active, service records are assigned to your organisation. You will receive a notification when a new record arrives. Navigate to the relevant service in your dashboard to view and begin working the case.
