# Service Provider Workflow Guide

This guide covers best practices and procedures for service providers processing cases in PartnerFlow — from receiving an assignment through to closing the case and ensuring customer satisfaction.

## Receiving a Service Record

When a service record is assigned to your organisation, you receive an email and in-app notification. Open the record to review:

- Customer information and contact details
- Product details and issue description
- Uploaded photos, receipts, and supporting documents
- Applicable warranty terms and coverage

Before proceeding, verify warranty eligibility, assess the complexity of the case, and confirm you have the parts and tools required.

## Customer Communication

### First Contact

Contact the customer within 24 hours of receiving the assignment.

- Introduce yourself and your service centre
- Confirm the reported issue
- Schedule an appointment or arrange collection
- Set clear expectations for the service timeline

Use the in-platform messaging or your connected email integration for all communications — this keeps a complete history on the record.

### Information Gathering

Collect the following if not already in the record:

- Product serial number and model
- Purchase date and location
- Detailed description of the issue
- Photos or video of the problem
- Any previous repair attempts

## Service Process

### Diagnostic Assessment

**On-site or in-shop:**
1. Visual inspection for obvious issues
2. Functional testing
3. Use manufacturer-approved diagnostic tools if applicable
4. Photograph findings and add them to the record
5. Record your assessment in the relevant custom fields

**Remote assessment (if applicable):**
1. Conduct a video call session with the customer
2. Walk the customer through basic troubleshooting
3. Document remote findings in the record

### Repair vs. Replacement Decision

Consider:

- Cost of repair relative to replacement
- Parts availability
- Warranty terms and manufacturer guidelines
- Customer preference

Document your recommendation in the service record with supporting evidence (photos, cost analysis).

### Repair Process

1. **Order parts** — raise a purchase order through PartnerFlow if needed
2. **Follow manufacturer repair procedures** — use approved tools and techniques
3. **Document each step** — photos, part numbers, procedures followed
4. **Test thoroughly** — verify all functions work correctly before closing

### Replacement Process

1. Obtain manufacturer approval for replacement via the transition flow
2. Confirm replacement product specifications
3. Coordinate the exchange with the customer
4. Collect the defective product and provide the replacement
5. Update the record with serial numbers and exchange details

## Updating Stage Status

Keep the record up to date as work progresses. Required status points:

| Stage | What to log |
|---|---|
| Initial contact | Confirm customer was contacted and appointment scheduled |
| Assessment complete | Diagnosis findings and recommendation |
| Repair/Replacement approved | Confirm approach and expected timeline |
| Parts ordered | Part numbers and expected delivery date |
| Service in progress | Work started, any interim findings |
| Service complete | Summary of work done, test results |
| Customer notified | Confirm customer was informed and satisfied |

Update the record within 4 hours of any status change. Include relevant notes and photos at key milestones.

## Documentation Requirements

### Assessment Documentation
- Diagnostic findings and photos
- Test results
- Parts identification
- Cost estimate justification

### Repair Documentation
- Parts used with part numbers
- Procedures followed
- Before and after photos
- Final testing results

### Photo Guidelines
- Clear, well-lit images
- Show the serial number where visible
- Clearly document damage or issues
- Include progress photos for significant repairs
- Final product condition after service

## Quality Assurance

### Before Service
- Verify warranty coverage
- Confirm customer expectations
- Review manufacturer repair procedures

### During Service
- Follow safety and compliance procedures
- Document all work as you go
- Test functionality at each significant step

### After Service
- Run full functional tests
- Verify the repair or replacement meets manufacturer standards
- Complete all documentation
- Contact the customer and confirm satisfaction

## Service Completion

1. Run final tests and verify all functions work correctly
2. Demonstrate the repaired or replaced product to the customer
3. Provide any care or usage instructions
4. Request a satisfaction rating through the platform
5. Close the record by completing the final transition

### Follow-up

Contact the customer within 48 hours of completion to verify continued satisfaction. Address any remaining concerns promptly and log the follow-up in the record.

## Tips for Efficient Case Management

- Use email templates for common customer communications to save time
- Set up notification preferences so you never miss an update
- Complete documentation as you work — don't leave it until the end
- Proactively communicate delays rather than waiting for a customer to chase
- Use the platform's task list features to manage internal assignments within your team
