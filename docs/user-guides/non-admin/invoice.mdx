# Invoices

As a service provider, invoices are generated automatically as you progress service records through their stages. You do not need to manually calculate or create invoices — PartnerFlow builds them from the pricing rules configured in the service flow.

## Where to Find Your Invoices

Navigate to **Accounts → Invoices** to view all invoices associated with your organisation.

Each invoice shows:

- The service record ID(s) included
- A line-by-line breakdown of charges (base fee, per-item charges, transition fees, logistics)
- The billing period covered
- Current status: Draft, Sent, Approved, or Paid

## How Invoices Are Generated

Invoices are created automatically based on the **Billing Period** setting in the service:

- **Per service** — an invoice is generated as soon as a service record reaches its final completed stage
- **Monthly** — all completed records within the month are bundled into a single invoice at month end

You do not need to take any action to generate an invoice. When a record completes, the invoice is created and made available in your Accounts section.

## Reviewing an Invoice

1. Open **Accounts → Invoices**
2. Click an invoice to view the full breakdown
3. Each charge line is traceable to a specific service record and the transition or stage that triggered it
4. The associated service record ID is listed — click it to open the full case history for reference

## Querying an Invoice

If you believe a charge is incorrect:

1. Open the invoice and note the affected line item and service record ID
2. Open the service record to review the stage history and transitions taken
3. Contact the service creator through **Partners → [Creator Name]** or via the in-platform messaging
4. The creator can issue a credit note or revised invoice if a correction is required

## Invoice Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Generated but not yet sent to the manufacturer for approval |
| **Sent** | Submitted to the manufacturer for review and approval |
| **Approved** | The manufacturer has confirmed the invoice |
| **Paid** | Payment has been processed |

## Payment

Payment terms are agreed in your contract with the manufacturer (typically 30–60 days from approval). Payment is processed outside PartnerFlow unless a direct payment integration has been configured by the manufacturer.
