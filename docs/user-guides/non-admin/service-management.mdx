# Service Records

Service records are the backbone of PartnerFlow. Each record represents a single case — a specific customer's product or item moving through a service flow. All stages, documentation, communications, assigned parts, and accounting for that case are tracked in one place.

## How Service Records Are Created

Service records can be generated in two ways:

### Manual Creation

A service creator or provider manually inputs the relevant details:

1. Navigate to the relevant service in your dashboard
2. Click **New Record**
3. Fill in the required fields (customer details, service item, initial data)
4. Save — the record is created at the first stage

### Automated Creation via Integration

Records can be triggered automatically from external systems:

- **CRM integration** — a new case in your CRM creates a corresponding service record in PartnerFlow
- **Customer portal form** — a customer submits a form and the system creates the record automatically
- **API call** — your system sends a request to the PartnerFlow API to create the record programmatically

Automated creation removes manual data entry and ensures records are opened the moment a customer need is identified.

## Progressing a Service Record

Once a record is open:

1. The current stage is displayed prominently
2. Available transitions appear based on Before conditions being met
3. Click a transition to advance — you may be prompted to fill in During requirements first
4. The record moves to the next stage and After automations fire automatically

All stage movements are logged with a timestamp and the user who triggered them.

## Sharing Service Records with Providers

Service records can be assigned to service provider partners:

### Manual Assignment

The service creator selects the provider from the record view:

1. Open the service record
2. Click **Assign Provider**
3. Select from your connected partner organisations
4. Save — the provider receives a notification

### Automated Assignment via Service Builder

The system can automatically select the most suitable provider based on criteria configured in the service flow:

- Specialisation or expertise
- Current capacity and workload
- Geographic proximity to the customer or service location

Automated assignment reduces delays and ensures the best-fit provider always gets the case.

## What Providers See

Once assigned, the service provider can access everything needed to carry out the service:

- All stage and transition information
- Custom fields and their current values
- Attached documentation and guides
- Inventory associated with the case
- Communication history with the customer

The manufacturer retains full visibility of progress throughout.

## Benefits

| Benefit | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Centralisation** | All service data, documents, communications, parts, and billing in one accessible record |
| **Efficiency** | Automated creation and assignment reduce manual work and errors |
| **Transparency** | Creators and providers share the same view — clear accountability at every stage |
| **Flexibility** | Dynamic assignment criteria route cases to the best-suited provider automatically |
