# Support

If you need help with PartnerFlow, the following resources and contact options are available.

## Self-Service Resources

Before contacting support, check these resources — most common questions are answered here:

- **[How It Works](/docs/getting-started/how-it-works)** — platform overview and key concepts
- **[FAQ](/docs/faq)** — answers to the most common questions
- **[API Reference](/docs/api/warrantyflow-v-3-api)** — full REST API documentation for developers
- **[Getting Started Guides](/docs/getting-started/how-it-works)** — step-by-step setup and first service walkthroughs

## Contact Support

### Email Support

For non-urgent questions, billing enquiries, and general assistance:

[support@warrantyflow.com](mailto:support@warrantyflow.com)

Response time: within 1 business day.

### Technical Issues

For platform bugs, integration problems, or data concerns:

[technical@warrantyflow.com](mailto:technical@warrantyflow.com)

Please include:
- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce it
- Your company name and account ID
- Any relevant screenshots or error messages

### Enterprise and Account Management

For enterprise customers with a dedicated account manager, contact your assigned representative directly. Their details are available in your onboarding documentation.

## When to Contact Support vs. Your Partner

| Issue | Who to contact |
|---|---|
| Can't access the platform / login issues | PartnerFlow Support |
| Bug or unexpected platform behaviour | PartnerFlow Support |
| Questions about a specific service or case | Your manufacturer / service creator |
| Invoice dispute | Your manufacturer / service creator |
| Integration setup assistance | PartnerFlow Technical Support |
| Billing and subscription changes | PartnerFlow Support |

## Reporting a Security Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in the platform, please report it responsibly:

[security@warrantyflow.com](mailto:security@warrantyflow.com)

Do not post security vulnerabilities publicly. We aim to acknowledge all reports within 48 hours.

## Support Hours

Monday to Friday, 8 AM – 6 PM GMT.

Outside of these hours, email support is monitored for critical issues. Enterprise customers with SLA agreements may have extended support hours as specified in their contract.
