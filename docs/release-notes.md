# Release Notes

This page tracks significant platform updates, new features, and improvements. Updates are listed in reverse chronological order.

---

## v1.0.0 — March 2026

**Initial platform release.**

### Service Management
- Service Flow Builder with visual stage and transition editor
- Before / During / After transition conditions
- Custom fields (text, number, date, dropdown, file upload)
- Service items with per-item and fixed-price transition pricing
- Service options: delivery model, customer association, billing period, inventory, logistics

### Partner Network
- Partner directory and connection requests
- Service sharing with configurable access levels
- Digital contract generation (SLA, SOW, MSA) from flow definitions
- E-signature and countersign workflow with email verification

### Service Records
- Manual and automated record creation
- Stage progression with audit trail
- Manual and automated provider assignment
- Customer portal for tracking, approvals, and payments

### Inventory
- Admin product catalogue with SKU, EAN, and category management
- Per-service inventory tracking for providers
- Purchase order creation and tracking
- Low-stock threshold alerts and automated draft purchase orders

### Accounting
- Automatic estimate generation with customer approval flow
- Automatic invoice generation from service transitions
- Per-service and monthly billing period options
- Full audit trail on all financial records

### Integrations
- Gmail OAuth connection for email notifications and replies
- Webhook support for automation triggers on transitions
- REST API (v3) with full CRUD coverage across all platform domains

### Administration
- Role-based access control with granular permissions
- Custom roles
- Audit log for all user and system activity
- Multi-user company accounts with invitation flow
