# Sharing Services

PartnerFlow makes it simple to share services with partners. Whether you are working with a new provider or collaborating with an existing one, the invitation, sharing, and acceptance process is designed to be fast while maintaining full transparency and security.

## Invite a New Partner

If you are in discussions with a service provider who is not yet on PartnerFlow, send them a direct invitation from the platform:

1. Go to **Partners → Directory**
2. Click **Invite Partner**
3. Enter the partner's company name and contact email
4. Send the invitation — they receive an email with onboarding instructions

Once they complete registration, they appear in your partner list and you can begin sharing services.

## Share a Service

Services can be shared from two places:

- **From the Service Builder** — open your service, go to **Service → Sharing**, select partners and configure access
- **From the Partners section** — navigate to **Partners → Shared Services** and select which services to share with which partners

When sharing, you can configure:
- Which team members at the partner organisation can access the service
- Effective dates for the partnership (start and end)
- Whether the partner can see pricing details

## Partner Accepts the Service

When a service is shared, the partner sees it in their **Partners → Awaiting Approval** list. Before accepting, they can access:

- The full stage and transition structure
- Documentation files attached to the service
- Contract terms and pricing

**Acceptance workflow:**

1. Partner reviews the service and provides any comments or clarification requests
2. Both sides align on terms
3. Partner signs digitally within PartnerFlow
4. A secondary verification code is sent to the partner's registered email for compliance
5. The service creator countersigns to finalise the agreement
6. The service is now live — the partner can begin receiving and executing service records

## Managing an Active Partnership

Once a service is accepted, both parties have ongoing access to the service's documentation and records. You can:

- Update documentation or service options at any time
- Issue a revised contract if terms change (requires re-signing)
- Revoke access if the partnership ends (Settings → Partners → Manage Access)

## Benefits

| Benefit | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Faster onboarding** | Invite and connect partners directly in the platform — no separate contract tooling |
| **Transparency** | Partners see all relevant details before accepting |
| **Secure approval** | Built-in verification ensures signatures are valid and agreements are protected |
| **Collaboration-ready** | Once accepted, services are immediately ready to execute |
